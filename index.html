Our Philosophy

Woven Excellence.

Vishal Textiles is a leading textile manufacturing company dedicated to producing premium-quality woven fabrics with precision, innovation, and reliability. With a modern fleet of advanced airjet weaving machines, we specialize in high-quality greige fabrics for domestic and international markets.

Our commitment to excellence, consistent quality standards, and customer satisfaction has helped us build strong relationships across the textile value chain — combining advanced technology, skilled craftsmanship, and efficient production to deliver fabrics that meet the evolving requirements of garment manufacturers, exporters, and processing units.

Our Journey.

From four looms and a family dream to a multi-unit textile powerhouse.

2002

The Founding

Two brothers and their father began with a dream and only 4 power loom machines — a small family venture built on hard work and vision.

Growth Years

Expansion

Through dedication, quality work, and the trust of customers, the business expanded into multiple manufacturing units — growing to 150+ power looms across facilities.

Technology Leap

Airjet Division

As technology advanced, the company embraced modern weaving solutions — launching an airjet weaving division that now operates 60+ machines delivering superior efficiency and consistency.

Today

10 Lakh Metres Monthly

From 4 looms to producing over 10 lakh metres of fabric every month — serving customers across domestic and international markets with reliable quality and timely delivery.
