Our Journey.

From four looms and a family dream to a multi-unit textile powerhouse.

2002

The Founding

Two brothers and their father began with a dream and only 4 power loom machines — a small family venture built on hard work and vision.

Growth Years

Expansion

Through dedication, quality work, and the trust of customers, the business expanded — growing to 150+ power looms across multiple facilities.

Technology Leap

Airjet Division

The company embraced modern weaving solutions — launching an airjet weaving division that now operates 60+ machines delivering superior efficiency and consistency.

Why Choose Vishal Textiles?

From a small beginning in 2002 with just four power looms, we have grown into a trusted textile manufacturing group with multiple units and more than 150 weaving machines. We produce over 10 lakh metres of quality fabric every month and continue to expand our capabilities.

By joining Vishal Textiles, you become part of a company that values hard work, innovation, teamwork, and continuous learning. We provide a supportive work environment where employees can develop their skills, take on new responsibilities, and build long-term careers. Our commitment to quality, growth, and employee development makes Vishal Textiles a great place to grow professionally and contribute to the future of the textile industry.
